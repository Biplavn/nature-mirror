# Nature Mirror - Concept Document

## Overview

Nature Mirror is an interactive augmented reality experience that brings nature to life through your screen. Using real-time hand tracking, users can attract and interact with realistic 3D creatures that respond naturally to their movements.

## Core Concept

**"Bring the magic of nature into your digital space"**

Nature Mirror creates an enchanting experience where users see themselves on screen surrounded by beautiful creatures that react to their presence. The creatures follow hand movements, creating an engaging and meditative interaction.

## Target Experience

### Primary Interactions
- **Hand Tracking**: Creatures are attracted to the user's hands
- **Natural Movement**: Creatures move smoothly and realistically
- **Spatial Awareness**: Creatures maintain proper spacing (no clustering)
- **Responsive Behavior**: Creatures react to sudden movements (startle response)

### Creature Types

1. **Hummingbirds**
   - Hover with characteristic figure-8 wing pattern
   - Quick, darting movements
   - Curious and bold personality
   - Head tracks user movement

2. **Fish (Tropical)**
   - Smooth swimming with body undulation
   - Schooling behavior when idle
   - Various species (clownfish, blue tang, etc.)
   - Realistic fin animations

3. **Bees**
   - Characteristic zigzag flight pattern
   - Buzzy, energetic movement
   - Swarm cohesion when no target
   - Waggle dance behavior

## User Experience Goals

1. **Magical**: The experience should feel enchanting and surprising
2. **Natural**: Creature movements should feel believable
3. **Responsive**: Creatures react immediately to user actions
4. **Relaxing**: Creates a calming, meditative atmosphere
5. **Accessible**: Works with any webcam, no special hardware

## Use Cases

- **Home relaxation**: Ambient nature experience
- **Children's entertainment**: Educational and engaging
- **Waiting rooms**: Calming environment for medical/dental offices
- **Retail displays**: Interactive storefront attractions
- **Events**: Photo booth experiences with nature themes
- **Meditation**: Mindful interaction with virtual nature

## Key Differentiators

1. **Real-time hand tracking** without special hardware
2. **High-quality 3D creatures** with realistic animations
3. **Natural flocking/schooling** behavior
4. **Smooth, non-jarring** movement (position-based collision)
5. **Multiple creature types** for variety
